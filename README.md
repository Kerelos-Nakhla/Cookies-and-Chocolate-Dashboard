🍪 Cookie Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI
📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard designed to analyze sales, profitability, product performance, and regional trends for a cookie business.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights, helping decision-makers monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and evaluate profitability across products and locations.

🎯 Business Problem

Businesses generate large volumes of sales data, but without effective reporting it becomes difficult to answer key business questions:

Are sales growing over time?
Which products drive the most revenue?
Which regions perform best?
Are sales targets being achieved?
How profitable is the business?

This dashboard provides a centralized view of business performance to support data-driven decision-making.

📊 Dashboard KPIs
KPI	Value

🔍 Key Insights
Revenue Growth
Revenue increased from approximately $1.1M in 2019 to $3.6M in 2020.
Top Products
Chocolate Chip Cookies generated the highest revenue.
White Chocolate Macadamia ranked second.
Regional Performance
Wisconsin was the highest-performing state.
New York followed closely behind.
Profitability
The business achieved a strong 57.94% profit margin.
